# Contoso Sales Analysis and Dashboard
This group project requires us to use the original Contoso Sales Samples file and perform additional analysis, applying both diagnostic and predictive analytics techniques.

**Data**: [Contoso Sales](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1TzZk_GsSfgNmQ511nZDRHn8aktajgXTG/view?usp=drive_link)

**Purpose**: 
1. Identify the sales channel contributing the highest revenue.
2. Determine stores that require performance improvement or attention.
3. Analyze products to identify those with the highest and lowest sales.
4. Evaluate the impact of promotions or discounts on sales performance.
5. Forecast next yearâ€™s sales assuming a 6% growth based on historical data.
